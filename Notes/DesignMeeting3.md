Design Overview
----------
  - because of our updated requirements, the enclosure needs to be completely redesigned
    - instead of camera being at the base of the camera, it will be located on the front end
        - actual lens will be protruding from the outside 
            - therefore, the part of the camera outside of the enclusure may need to be covered by another part of the enclosure 
            - rain is a problem; need to brainstorm different moisture-resistant ideas
        - camera will need to be mounted in a custom covering inside the enclosure since the circuit board of the camera is fragile
    - Beaglebone Black can be mounted with standoffs at the bottom of the enclosure
    - mBed can be placed next to BBB since there will be enough space due to the size of the solar battery
        - one option of mounting may be a mini breadboard next to the BBB since mBed has its pins protruding from the bottom
        - other option is always to hot glue it on!
    - solar battery can be mounted on the top of the enclosure
        - solar panel must be open to environment to get sunlight
            - this means that enclosure must have an opening at the top
            - also means that the opening must be water-tight when the solar batter is placed in its position
        - also may need a custom covering inside the enclosure to ensure it remains tight to the top of the enclosure
  - started SolidWorks construction of various components as well as the enclosure
